Community.

There are several opportunities available for our community to be part of UNBLOKC.

PARTNER

Community Partners collaborate with The BLOKC to enhance the impact and inclusivity of UNBLOKC by leveraging diverse perspectives, resources, and networks. Together, they create a lasting positive impact on the community.

BLOKCMATE

BLOKCmates are mentees seeking professional connections in Web3 and The BLOKC community. Paired with a Mentor, they receive insight, advice, and opportunities to build lasting connections with influential community members.

MENTOR

Mentors provide BLOKCmates with support, guidance, and professional networking at UNBLOKC conferences. They should be familiar with Web3 and the community, create networking opportunities, and assist BLOKCmates in navigating the conference.
